How to Use

  1. Position phone directly above drape tester
  2. Place a reference coin next to drape for scale
  3. Ensure good lighting and contrast
  4. Click "Start Camera" or "Upload Image"
  5. After capture, click on the reference coin
  6. Enter drape tester diameters and click "Calculate Drape %"
